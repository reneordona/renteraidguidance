Long Beach Renter Aid Program

The following guidance is provided by the Public Affairs Office for staff who may be approached by media or community members regarding this program.

About the Program
What the Program Provides
The Long Beach Renter Aid Program provides short-term financial assistance to low-income renters at risk of losing their homes.

Payments are made directly to landlords or utility companies. Participants may also be connected to food assistance, healthcare, and other essential services.

The program prioritizes seniors (55+), households facing eviction, and those impacted by income loss or federal benefit changes.
